Process

I decided to redesign my logo when constructing this website. I created a mockup of what the website was going to look like to give me guide when building this site.

My goal was to create a much more colorful and cuter giraffe than my previous designs.

I quickly sketched a Giraffe at the top of the site mockup, and used it to begin the process of iterating through designs.

I love Giraffes.


I did a few quick sketches of the character, trying to be as loose as possible with my drawing as to not get caught up in details. Being as loose as possible allowed me to exaggerate some forms, for a more cartoony look.

Extremely rough sketches.


I experimented with a few different design ideas. Experimenting with different kinds of eyes, whether to include glasses, and what style the eyes would be drawn in.

A few glasses ideas, and some anime eyes for fun.


I started settling on the final design, and began touching up and polishing the line art to prepare for painting.

Final touches to the line art.


As I first decided in my initial idea for the design, the idea was for the logo to be cartoony and colorful. For this, I went with a Cel-Shading technique, and stuck with soft colors for a cartoony look.
